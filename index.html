About

This website serves several purposes:

  • To document Ryan's webdev learnings as he assesses software engineering as a career path.
  • To organize and chronicle various Dungeons & Dragons campaigns for which Ryan is the Dungeon Master.
  • To host various Dungon Master tools Ryan has created to make DMing and playing D&D easier.
  • To detail the mythical Celtic-inspired world of mag Ildathach, created by Ryan for the primary purpose of facilitating various Dungeons & Dragons campaigns.
